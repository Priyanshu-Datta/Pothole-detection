About This Project

Road Infrastructure Assessment

This AI system helps municipalities and transportation departments quickly assess road conditions and identify maintenance priorities. By analyzing images of road surfaces, the model classifies pavement conditions into four categories, enabling data-driven maintenance decisions.

How It Works:

  1. Upload a clear image of a road surface
  2. The AI analyzes the image in real-time
  3. Receive instant classification and recommendations
  4. View prediction history and statistics
  5. Use insights for maintenance planning
